Volunteering

I believe that volunteering can act as a catalyst for positive societal change. Since 2015 I have participated in numerous, mostly science-related, events assuming various roles - from referee in robotic competitions to organizational team member of the biggest science festival in Greece.

Mentoring

The insights that a mentor provides can be incredibly empowering. We rise by lifting others - I have had amazing mentors myself, and I am eager to share my own experiences with anyone who might benefit. Right now I am registered as a mentor with Greek Women in STEM, and I am always up for a chat.
