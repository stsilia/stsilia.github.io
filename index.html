About Me

I’m currently pursuing my PhD at the Instituto de Astrofísica de Canarias, focusing on the formation and evolution of substellar mass objects — like brown dwarfs and exoplanets. I’m passionate about understanding the universe at both large and small scales, and I also enjoy science communication and outreach.

When I’m not looking at stars (or code), you can probably find me sketching, walking by the sea, or exploring new coffee spots around the islands.
